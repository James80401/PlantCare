# Plant Buddy — Species Roster

## Overview

There are 12 plant species available as buddies. Each has a unique visual design, personality trait pool, idle animation set, dialogue flavor, grow stage appearances, and unlock condition. The player chooses one on first launch (onboarding) and can unlock others over time.

---

## Species 1 — Cactus "Spike"

**Unlock:** Default (available from launch)
**Personality:** Stoic, dry humor, independent
**Trait Pool:** RESILIENT, WILD
**Dialogue Flavor:** Understated, matter-of-fact, occasionally cutting
**Example Quote:** "I don't need much. But I notice when you forget."

**Idle Animations:**
- Slow sway left and right every 4–6 seconds
- Occasional spine glint (small highlight flash on tips)
- Rare flower bloom animation on top of head (triggers after 3 consecutive care days)

**Unique Mechanic:** Mood degrades 40% slower than other species. Spike hides when he's unhappy — good poker face.

**Grow Stages:**
1. Seed — tiny nub barely above soil
2. Sprout — small barrel shape, no arms
3. Seedling — taller barrel, one arm stub appearing
4. Young Plant — classic barrel cactus with two arms
5. Established — tall saguaro shape, multiple arms
6. Ancient — massive multi-armed ancient saguaro with blooms

---

## Species 2 — Monstera "Monty"

**Unlock:** Default (available from launch)
**Personality:** Cheerful, social, dramatic
**Trait Pool:** SUN_SEEKER, TENDER
**Dialogue Flavor:** Enthusiastic, a little extra, loves attention
**Example Quote:** "New hole in my leaf! It's called fenestration, not damage, thank you."

**Idle Animations:**
- Leaves fan out slowly when mood is happy
- Theatrical drooping when mood is sad or thirsty
- New leaf unfurl animation triggers on stage advancement

**Unique Mechanic:** Visually grows fastest — new leaf animations are the most frequent of all species. Great for users who want to see visible progress quickly.

**Grow Stages:**
1. Seed — single tiny heart-shaped leaf on a thin stem
2. Sprout — two small leaves, no fenestration yet
3. Seedling — three leaves, first split appearing
4. Young Plant — five leaves, visible fenestration
5. Established — full bushy monstera with multiple fenestrated leaves
6. Ancient — enormous monstera with aerial roots hanging down

## Species 5 — Sunflower "Sunny"

**Unlock:** Achieve a 7-day care streak
**Personality:** Enthusiastic, energetic, loud
**Trait Pool:** SUN_SEEKER, WILD
**Dialogue Flavor:** ALL CAPS sometimes, exclamation points, very excited about everything
**Example Quote:** "GOOD MORNING!! Did you see the SUN today?? It's SO GOOD."

**Idle Animations:**
- Head tracks toward brightest part of screen like heliotropism
- Constant subtle bouncing — Sunny cannot stay still
- Seeds float off gently during idle moments
- Extra-expressive face with full eyebrow animations

**Unique Mechanic:** Sunlight bar fills 20% faster. Sunny converts enthusiasm into XP.

**Grow Stages:**
1. Seed — tiny sprout just poking through soil
2. Sprout — short stem, two small leaves
3. Seedling — taller stem, visible bud forming
4. Young Plant — tall stem, half-open bloom, disc center showing
5. Established — full large sunflower in peak bloom
6. Ancient — giant sunflower taller than the pot is wide, surrounded by smaller volunteer sunflowers

## Species 6 — Fern "Fernie"

**Unlock:** Complete 5 consecutive watering tasks without missing one
**Personality:** Gentle, nurturing, slightly anxious
**Trait Pool:** TENDER, RESILIENT
**Dialogue Flavor:** Caring, asks questions, worries a little

**Idle Animations:**

**Unique Mechanic:** Friendship XP is doubled — Fernie grows faster when you send sunshine to friends. Community-oriented species.

**Grow Stages:**
1. Seed — single small frond just uncurling
2. Sprout — two fronds, slightly arching
3. Seedling — small cluster of four fronds
4. Young Plant — arching bushy cluster
5. Established — full lush fern
6. Ancient — cascading forest fern with fronds that fill the screen

## Species 12 — Mushroom "Mushi"

**Unlock:** Complete 10 tasks logged after 8:00 PM
**Personality:** Mysterious, nocturnal, oddly wise
**Trait Pool:** NIGHT_BLOOMER, WILD
**Dialogue Flavor:** Elliptical, cryptic, knows more than it lets on

**Idle Animations:**
- Bioluminescent glow pulses gently
- Spore puffs drift upward periodically
- Cap bobs on a flexible stem
- Extra glow during night hours (after 8 PM based on device time)

**Unique Mechanic:** All tasks logged after 8 PM earn 50% bonus XP. Ideal for night owl users.

**Grow Stages:**
1. Seed — tiny mycelium threads just visible in soil
2. Sprout — small button mushroom
3. Seedling — taller stem, cap forming
4. Young Plant — full capped mushroom, glowing softly
5. Established — cluster of glowing mushrooms
6. Ancient — full fairy ring of glowing mushrooms surrounding a giant central cap

## Database: BuddySpecies Model

```prisma
model BuddySpecies {
  id            String       @id  // "monstera", "cactus", "sunflower", etc.
  displayName   String
  description   String
  traitPool     BuddyTrait[]
  unlockType    UnlockType
  unlockValue   Int?         // dewdrop cost, streak count, etc.
  unlockCondition String?    // human-readable description
  isDefault     Boolean      @default(false)
  isSeasonal    Boolean      @default(false)
  seasonMonths  Int[]        // e.g. [9,10,11] for autumn
  growStageDescriptions Json  // array of 6 stage descriptions
  idleAnimations Json         // array of animation key strings
  uniqueMechanic String
  dialogueSamples Json        // array of sample quotes
}

enum UnlockType {
  DEFAULT
  DEWDROPS
  STREAK
  QUEST
  JOURNEY_COUNT
  SEASONAL
  TASK_CONDITION
}
```
