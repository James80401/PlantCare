|---------|---------|

**Change location** refreshes pending tasks for the new growing spot - [tutorial](../tutorials/changing-plant-location.md).

## Journal and Plant Life

The Journal tab separates three kinds of history:

- **Journal note:** a quick observation, optional photo, or growth measurement.
- **Care event:** a completed, skipped, or snoozed care task from the Tasks tab.
- **Plant Check-In:** a periodic health snapshot that Dr. Plant summarizes into
  the plant's Plant Life history.

Plant Check-Ins group answers by overall condition, leaves/growth, soil/water,
recent care, pests or concerns, and optional photo. Editing a Plant Check-In
refreshes its Dr. Plant summary. Deleting one removes that entry and updates the
Plant Life history.
